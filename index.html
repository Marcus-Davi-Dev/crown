About Ha Long Bay

Ha Long Bay, located in northern Vietnam, is one of the world's most stunning natural wonders. With over 1,600 limestone islands rising dramatically from emerald waters, it's a UNESCO World Heritage Site that captivates visitors with breathtaking beauty and adventure opportunities.
